In-person workshop • CHI 2026 • Barcelona, Spain

The Co-Living between Reality and Virtuality as a Daily Routine

Mixed Reality (MR) is quickly becoming part of everyday life — from AI-powered XR wearables to seamless transitions between physical and virtual environments. This workshop brings together researchers, designers, and practitioners to explore inclusive onboarding and interaction techniques for a future where moving across the reality–virtuality continuum becomes routine.
