Program

CHI 2026 Workshop • Barcelona, Spain

Tuesday, April 14 2026 [14:15 - 15:45 and 16:30 - 18:00 CET]

Click the room name to view it on the map.
P1 - Room 128 (3. Convention Centre - P1)

Two 90-minute sessions separated by a 45-minute break — 180 minutes total.

Time Duration Session
14:15 – 14:30 15 min
Welcome & Opening
Introduction to the workshop theme and framing of the day.
14:30 – 15:30 60 min
Lightning Round
Short presentations from accepted contributions, focused on everyday XR contexts.
15:30 – 15:45 15 min
Thematic Groups & Golden Rules Kick-off
Formation of thematic groups, introduction of the Golden Rules activity, and first group discussion to prepare the collaborative session.
15:45 – 16:30 45 min
Break — Coffee & Poster Walk
Informal discussion, networking, and exploration of paper connections.
16:30 – 16:50 20 min
Golden Rules Workstreams
Thematic groups develop shared technical and ethical principles for XR co-living.
16:50 – 17:00 10 min
Golden Rules Sharing
Short presentation of the rules developed by each group.
17:00 – 17:40 40 min
Persona Stress Test
Mixed groups apply the rules to concrete personas and identify tensions in practice.
17:40 – 17:55 15 min
Persona Sharing & Rule Revisions
Mixed groups present their personas, highlight tensions, and propose any new or revised Golden Rules emerging from the discussion.
17:55 – 18:00 5 min
Closing Remarks
Final reflections, collective wrap-up, and next steps.
Expected outcome
A public workshop synthesis bringing together practical and ethical guidance for future XR development in everyday life contexts.
